Overview

  • Reduced financial burden on tap dancing
  • Increased flexibility in shoe choices

Impact — For the Industry

  • More accessible tap dancing to wider audience
  • Innovative approach to traditional product
  • Growing market for affordable solutions

Research

Dancers face challenges with traditional tap shoes: high cost, difficulty in transportation, and the burden of carrying multiple pairs. Many dancers wish for a more flexible, cost-effective solution that doesn't compromise on performance.

Ideation

How might we create an affordable, portable tap shoe solution that enables dancers to reduce costs and carrying burden while maintaining performance quality?

Solution

EasyTap is a detachable tap shoe system that allows dancers to convert regular dance shoes into tap shoes, reducing cost and carrying burden while providing professional-grade performance.

Key Features

  • Detachable Design — easily attach and detach tap soles
  • Cost-Effective — reduce investment in multiple tap shoes
  • Lightweight — portable and easy to carry
  • Professional Sound — high-quality tap performance
  • Universal Compatibility — works with various dance shoes

Design Approach

The design prioritizes accessibility and affordability while maintaining the performance quality that professional and amateur dancers expect. Every detail was considered to balance functionality, cost, and user experience.
